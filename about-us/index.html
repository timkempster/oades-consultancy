About Oades Consultancy

A UK-based German legal consultancy founded on transparency, expertise, and a genuine commitment to helping clients navigate German law with confidence.

Who We Are

Oades Consultancy is a UK-based legal consultancy specialising in German law. We provide professional, fixed-fee advice to individuals and businesses across the UK and internationally who need expert guidance on German legal matters – without the barriers of language, geography, or unpredictable legal fees.

All of our consultations are conducted online, making specialist German legal expertise accessible to clients wherever they are in the world. We work with clients in the United Kingdom, Europe, North America, Australia, and beyond.

Our Founder – Robert Oades

Robert Oades is a British citizen who completed over eight years of German legal training and practical experience in Baden-Württemberg, one of Germany's most commercially active states. During that time, he worked across a broad range of German legal practice areas including employment law, tenancy disputes, tax matters, inheritance proceedings, and claims enforcement.

Robert founded Oades Consultancy with a clear mission: to give English-speaking clients straightforward access to reliable German legal knowledge, at a price they can predict from the outset.

Our Approach

We believe that good legal advice should be clear, affordable, and accessible. Every client receives a dedicated consultation with no vague estimates and no escalating hourly billing. Our fixed-fee model means you know exactly what you are paying before you book.

  • Fixed-fee consultations at £100 per hour, including all taxes
  • All services delivered online – no requirement to travel or attend in person
  • Plain-English explanations of complex German legal matters
  • Transparent cost estimates for any follow-on work
  • Clients can submit supporting documents before each session

Areas of Practice

We advise on a wide range of German legal matters including employment and tenancy disputes, tax assistance and refunds, visa and residence issues, inheritance and probate, asset tracing, claims enforcement, child support modifications, and German pension claims.

Please note: Oades Consultancy is not admitted as a Rechtsanwalt in Germany and does not provide legal representation in German courts or regulatory proceedings. We operate as a legal consultancy from the United Kingdom.
