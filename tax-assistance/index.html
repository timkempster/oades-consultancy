German Tax Assistance

Specialist guidance on German tax obligations, annual filings, and refund claims for UK-based and international individuals with German tax history.

German Tax Matters for Non-Residents

If you have worked in Germany, held German investments, or received income from a German source, you may have ongoing German tax obligations – or be entitled to a refund of taxes already paid. Navigating the German tax system from abroad, in a foreign language, can be a significant challenge.

Oades Consultancy provides clear, practical guidance on German tax matters for English-speaking clients, helping you understand your obligations and opportunities at a fixed and transparent price.

How We Can Help

  • Tax refund claims – identifying and recovering overpaid Lohnsteuer (wage tax) and other German taxes
  • Annual tax return guidance – advice on completing the German Einkommensteuererklärung as a non-resident or former resident
  • Understanding your liability – clarifying whether you have remaining German tax obligations and what they involve
  • Cross-border tax issues – guidance on the UK–Germany double taxation agreement and its implications for your situation
  • Employment income – advising on tax withheld from German employment, particularly for short-term or seasonal workers
  • Correspondence with German tax authorities – help understanding letters from the Finanzamt and advising on how to respond

Who Typically Uses This Service

Our tax assistance service is commonly used by individuals who have:

  • Worked in Germany for a period and returned to the UK or moved to another country
  • Had German wage tax (Lohnsteuer) deducted from their earnings
  • Received a letter from the Finanzamt they do not understand
  • German rental income or investment returns that may be taxable
  • An outstanding German tax assessment they need help responding to

Our Process

Book a one-hour consultation (£100 fixed fee). Before the session, send us any relevant documents – payslips, P60 equivalents, Finanzamt correspondence, or previous tax assessments. We will review everything in advance and use the consultation to explain your position clearly and set out your options. Any follow-on assistance is quoted separately before we proceed.

Please note: Oades Consultancy provides consultancy guidance on German tax matters. We are not a Steuerberater (German tax advisor) and do not file returns directly with German tax authorities on your behalf, though we can assist you in preparing and understanding what to submit.
