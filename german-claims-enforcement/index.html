German Claims Enforcement

If you are owed money by a party in Germany – whether an individual, a business, or a former employer – German law provides a clear framework for pursuing and enforcing that claim. The challenge for international creditors is navigating that framework from abroad, in a different language, and often without knowing where to start.

Oades Consultancy helps individuals and businesses assess their position and identify the strongest enforcement options available under German law. We provide clear, practical guidance on how to pursue your claim efficiently and professionally.

Who This Service Is For

  • Individuals owed money by a former employer in Germany
  • Businesses with unpaid invoices from German clients or partners
  • Landlords or tenants with unresolved financial claims from a German tenancy
  • Anyone seeking to recover a court-ordered payment from a debtor in Germany
  • Creditors who have received no response to informal payment requests

How We Can Help

Your initial enforcement assessment (£100 fixed fee) covers a review of your claim details and a clear outline of the options available to you under German law. We will explain what steps are realistic, what timescales to expect, and what costs may be involved in taking the matter further.

Depending on the circumstances, enforcement options under German law may include:

  • Formal demand letters (Mahnung) and pre-court procedures
  • Statutory interest and late-payment compensation, which accrue automatically under German law
  • The simplified court order procedure (Mahnverfahren) for undisputed debts
  • Debtor tracing where contact details are unknown or out of date
  • Guidance on engaging a Rechtsanwalt for court-based enforcement where required

Transparent Costs

Your initial consultation is £100, fixed, including all taxes. Any follow-on work is quoted separately and transparently before you commit. We maintain full cost control throughout, so there are no unexpected charges at any stage.

Please note: Oades Consultancy is not admitted as a Rechtsanwalt in Germany and cannot represent you in German court proceedings. Where court-based enforcement is required, we can advise on the process and assist you in engaging appropriate representation in Germany.
