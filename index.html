Our Story

About Oades Consultancy

Founded by Robert Oades, a British citizen with over eight years of German legal training and practical experience in Baden-Württemberg. We provide fixed-fee German legal consultancy online, accessible to clients worldwide.

Our approach is straightforward: expert advice, clear pricing, and dedicated support. Whether you are navigating employment law, recovering a pension, or dealing with an inheritance, we bring specialist knowledge to every matter.

Please note: Oades Consultancy is not admitted as a Rechtsanwalt in Germany and does not provide representation in German courts.
